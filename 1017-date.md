# date

```bash
date ===>> show date
date -u ===>> always be UTC
date +%y-%m-%d-%h:%M:%S
Y ===>> year
y ===>> only year
m ===>> mounth
d ===>> day
h ===>> hour
M ===>> minustes
S ===>> second
date +%s ===>> when linux burn (time stamp)
```
